E-commerce Survey Results by Keyna L. INAMUGISHA

The survey was done on a total of 30 people in order to find out how popular e-commerce websites are and explore the demographics of online shoppers.
Here are the findings:

1. How many have purchased an item online?
  -> 24
2. How many have shopped but not purchased online?
  -> 8
From question 3, i only focused on the 24 people who shop online
3. How many purchase online once a year? Once a month? Once a week?
4. What is their age range (18-25, 26-39, 40-50, or over 50)?
5. What is their gender?
6. What is their level of education (high school, some college, college graduate, or graduate school)?
7. What is their favorite online shopping site?

In my findings, I can conclude that most of the online shoppers are in the age category 18-25, and are college graduate or with some college experience.
The country they're located in also influenced their shopping behavior: how often they shop and which websites they shop on.
The gender has almost no effect in my survey, although it is said that men dominate the stats spending 28% more than women when shopping online.
In the Ecommerce Statistics for 2023, it is found that most online shoppers are Millenials and Gen Xers. They spend 50% more time shopping online than others.

Reference: Ecommerce Statistics for 2023
